Fruits are the edible parts of flowering plants that contain seeds. They are often sweet or sour in taste and are typically consumed as part of a healthy diet due to their rich nutritional content, including vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. Fruits come in various shapes, sizes, colors, and flavors, ranging from apples, oranges, and bananas to berries, melons, and tropical fruits like mangoes and pineapples.

Types of fruit

  • Citrus Fruits: Examples include oranges, lemons, limes, grapefruits, and tangerines
  • Berries: Examples include strawberries, blueberries, raspberries, blackberries, and cranberries.
  • Tropical Fruits: Examples include mangoes, pineapples, papayas, bananas, guavas, and passion fruits.
  • Stone Fruits: Examples include peaches, plums, cherries, apricots, and nectarines.
